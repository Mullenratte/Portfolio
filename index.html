Quokk-A-Mole (WIP)

Shooter & Endless Runner with unique twist

Quokk, a cheeky little rodent agent, and his mole friend are trying to infiltrate the Hornet's Nest, a notorious bandit outpost in the Dark Forest. Fight your way through, but be careful. Your actions might be your own demise!

Defeated enemies are disintegrated and their power is fed to the mole, which in turn gets stronger and faster. Watch out for any eggs dup up by his unsatiable hunger!

About Me

Hey!

I'm Max Blanc, a creative, enthusiastic game developer looking to make my way into the games industry. I'm passionate for designing innovative gameplay systems and bringing them to life.

Besides university, I spend my time working both on personal projects and on the Steam game Viking Chef: Feast Frenzy (Yew Rune Creations) to further hone my programming knowledge as well as other game dev related skills, such as pixel art and design principles.

The remaining time you'll either find me playing games myself, be it on PC or analog (DnD5e), or - when I've sat enough for the day - lifting weights and doing historical fencing (long sword) at Historisches Schwertfechten Nordhessen.

If you want to see more of my artistic endeavours regarding pixel art, please take a look at my Instagram.
